Our Mission

TestBedz was born out of a UMich innovation project with a simple goal: to break down the barriers to essential testing for student innovators and small startups. We believe that great ideas deserve a clear path to validation. This Alpha platform is our first step towards building a seamless ecosystem for testing and verification.
